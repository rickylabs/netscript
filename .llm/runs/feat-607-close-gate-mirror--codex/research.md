# Research — #607 close-gate evidence mirror

- The existing close-gate extracts closing-keyword issue numbers from the PR body, fetches those
  issues, and independently fails while scoped acceptance/gate checkboxes remain unchecked.
- The CI job currently has read-only issue permissions and runs the checker as its sole gate step.
- The safest composition is a conditional mirror step immediately before the existing checker in
  the same job; the checker remains unchanged as the final authority over issue state.
- `.agents/skills/` is authoritative; `.claude/skills/` is generated by
  `deno task agentic:sync-claude`.
